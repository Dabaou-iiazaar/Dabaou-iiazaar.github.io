Debate Team 2019-20

        

Feast your eyes on the formal debate team of Vincent Massey Secondary School, Massey Debate! With more than 10 years of students willing to put their spontaneous communication skills to the test every week, we have transformed students from being shy and quiet to being able to talk out of situations that seemed impossible to escape.

     

Communication, together.

No matter how good you think your communication skills may be, there is always room for improvement. With a partner, you can enhance your communication skills to levels that you had no idea you could reach. All it takes is commitment on a Tuesday afternoon. Not only does it help in oral communication, but also in fields where having general knowledge and critical thinking skills are important.
